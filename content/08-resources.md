Laser cut chassis template and 3D printable chassis, both designed in Fusion 360. Download the file that matches your fabrication method.

<!-- SLIDE_BREAK -->

### Laser cut chassis (DXF)

Designed for 3mm acrylic or MDF. All holes are sized for M3 hardware. Includes mounts for the motor:bit carrier board, IR sensors at the front, and the ultrasonic sensor.

### 3D printed chassis (STL)

Same geometry as the DXF, exported as a solid for FDM printing. Print at 0.2mm layer height, 20% infill, no supports needed.
